Returns
We accept returns within 10 days from the date of purchase.

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the refund status.
If your return is approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method.
Please note that shipping charges from the original purchase are non-refundable.

If we confirm that the item was out of stock at the time of your purchase, you will receive a 100% refund, as this is our responsibility.
The refund will be processed automatically, and you will be notified via email or phone.
